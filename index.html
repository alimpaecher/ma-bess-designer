Compliance & Eligibility Checklist

NFPA 855 — Energy Storage Installation [8]

  • ESS units separated by minimum 3 ft (Section 15.5)
  • 10 ft minimum separation to means of egress
  • 20 ft fire department vehicle access lane
  • Smoke/gas detection system required
  • Thermal monitoring system required
  • Fire suppression system (sprinkler or clean agent)
  • Fire-resistant barriers/compartments
  • UL 9540A large-scale fire testing data required
  • Signage per NFPA 855 requirements

MA SMART 3.0 Storage Eligibility [6]

  • Storage paired with solar (required)
  • Minimum 2-hour duration at rated capacity
  • Round-trip efficiency ≥ 70% (LFP: ~95%)
  • LiFePO4 technology (eligible)
  • System ≥ 1 MW for storage adder (SMART 3.0)
  • UL 9540 listed system

527 CMR 1.00 — MA Fire Safety Code [9]

  • Chapter 52 compliance (systems > 20 kWh)
  • Building permit (780 CMR) required
  • Fire official permit (527 CMR 1.00) required
  • Wiring inspector permit (527 CMR 12.00) required
  • Inverters listed per UL 1741 or UL 9540
  • Local fire department review & approval
